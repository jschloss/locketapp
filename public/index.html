Introducing Locket.
A faster, safer way to sign in.

Knock on your iPhone twice — even if it's in your pocket — and your Mac unlocks. Walk away, and your Mac locks automatically. Every time. No pins, no passwords, no gimmicks. You don't even have to open the app on your phone.

More secure than a password.
Without the password.

  • Proximity Lock

    Locket locks your Mac the moment you walk away, virtually eliminating unwanted access to your computer.

  • Imposter Check

    Before unlocking, Locket makes sure your unlock screen isn't being spoofed by a keystroke logger.

  • Encrypted Entry

    Locket automatically enters your password with military-grade 2048-bit RSA encryption.

  • Password Backup

    Lost or stolen iPhone? You can still sign in with your password and easily unpair your Locket.

Best friends with your battery.

Locket talks to your Mac with Bluetooth Low Energy, a new technology that draws very little power. You won’t see any impact on your phone’s battery life, even if you use Locket all day, every day.

Maybe for more than just your Mac.

We'd like to live in a world without passwords. A world where your stuff just works when you try to use it, and doesn't work for anyone else.

We think Locket just might be a way to get there. Think of your Locket as the keycard to everything in your life, only it can’t be stolen. And it works only for you.
